HooRay Bakery started with a simple belief — that the best things are made with care, in small batches, for the people you love.

Based in Irvine, CA, we bake Japanese-style milk breads and artisan treats using quality ingredients, no preservatives, and a whole lot of heart. Every loaf is made to order.
